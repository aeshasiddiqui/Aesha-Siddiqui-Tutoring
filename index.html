About

A calm, structured plan for real academic growth.

Brainwave Tutoring helps families turn school struggles into clear next steps. Each student starts with a conversation and an academic assessment, then receives a customized learning plan that fits their goals, pace, and confidence level.

Sessions can focus on intervention, homework support, test preparation, skill building, or enrichment. Weekly progress reports and optional homework help families see what is improving and what still needs attention.

Services Provided

Tutoring for the skills students use every day.

Phonics Intervention

Sound-letter practice, decoding, fluency, and early reading routines for students who need stronger foundations.

Dyslexia Support

Patient, structured reading help using clear steps, repeated practice, and confidence-building strategies.

K-8 Math Tutoring

Number sense, operations, fractions, word problems, pre-algebra, homework review, and test preparation.

ADHD & Executive Functioning

Coaching for planning, organization, study routines, task follow-through, and schoolwork independence.

Homeschool Support

Supplemental lessons, subject support, weekly structure, and skill checks for homeschool families.

State Test Preparation

Review sessions for grade-level reading and math standards, practice questions, pacing, and test-day confidence.

SAT Preparation

Focused review for SAT reading, writing, math, timing, question strategy, and practice test reflection.

Gifted Student Enrichment

Challenging reading, writing, math, and project-based learning for students ready to stretch beyond classwork.
